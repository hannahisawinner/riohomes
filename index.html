About Us

At RIO, we believe everyone deserves the chance to own a home, even if you don't have perfect credit or can't get approved by a bank. We're a local real estate company, based in San Antonio, and we specialize in buying homes using creative financing that helps homeowners in all kinds of situations - whether you recently bought a home and now owe more than it's worth, are going through a divorce, are facing foreclosure, or just need to move fast. We offer real solutions when the traditional route doesn't work.

We also work with future buyers who are tired of the bank runaround. If you've been told "No" because of credit scores or past financial struggles, we offer a different way forward. With RIO, you can work toward owning a home without dealing with banks, credit checks, or long approval processes.

Our mission is to make homeownership possible for more people: real solutions for real lives.
