About Us

At RIO, we believe everyone should have a chance to own a home, even if the bank says no. We help people sell or buy a home in many tough situations:

  • Owe more than your home is worth
  • Need to sell quickly
  • Facing foreclosure
  • Struggling with credit

With RIO, you can work toward owning a home without banks, credit checks, or long approval waits. Let’s make homeownership possible, together.
