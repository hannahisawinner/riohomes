About Us

At RIO, we believe everyone should have a chance to own a home — even if the bank says no. We're a local real estate company in San Antonio. We help people in all kinds of situations:

  • Owe more than your house is worth
  • Going through a divorce
  • Facing foreclosure
  • Need to sell quickly

We buy homes using creative financing — no perfect credit or bank approval needed. We also help future buyers who are:

  • Tired of dealing with banks
  • Struggling with credit
  • Working through past financial issues

With RIO, you can work toward owning a home without banks, credit checks, or long approval waits. Let’s make homeownership possible, together.
